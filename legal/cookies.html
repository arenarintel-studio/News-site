Cookies Policy

Last Updated: 12th April, 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to site owners. Arenar Intel uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and understand how our site is used.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to ensure the website functions properly, understand how visitors interact with our content, remember your preferences, and improve site performance and speed.

Types Of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies. These cookies are necessary for the website to function. They enable basic features such as page navigation and secure access. The site cannot function properly without them.

Analytics Cookies. These cookies help us understand how visitors use Arenar Intel. They collect anonymous information about pages visited, time spent on the site, and referral sources. We use this data to improve our content and user experience.

Preference Cookies. These cookies remember choices you make, such as language or display preferences, to enhance your experience on return visits.

Third-Party Cookies. Some content on our site, such as embedded social media posts or links to external sources, may set cookies from third-party sites. We do not control these cookies. Please review the relevant third-party cookie policies

How Long Cookies Stay

Session Cookies are temporary and expire when you close your browser. Persistent Cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them manually.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of Arenar Intel and other websites you visit.

Instructions for managing cookies in common browsers:

Google Chrome: Settings, Privacy and Security, Cookies and other site data.

Mozilla Firefox: Options, Privacy and Security, Cookies and Site Data.

Apple Safari: Preferences, Privacy, Manage Website Data.

Microsoft Edge: Settings, Site Permissions, Cookies and site data.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Changes take effect immediately upon posting. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
