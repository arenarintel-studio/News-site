About Arenar Intel

Arenar Intel, also known as Arenar Intelligence, is a global intelligence feed delivering verified, sourced and direct information on the events shaping our world.

We cover geopolitics, finance, crypto, Africa, BRICS, and technology with one commitment: no noise, no speculation, no opinion. Just the signal.

Our Mission

The world moves fast. Misinformation moves faster. Arenar Intel exists to cut through the fog and provide clarity. Every story we publish is attributed to a named, verifiable source. We do not publish rumors. We do not chase clicks. We provide Intelligence you can trust.

Why Arenar Intel Is Different

Traditional outlets rely on long-form reporting, anonymous briefings, and narrative framing. Arenar Intel does not.

We deliver short-form intelligence designed for the way people consume information today. Every word earns its place. Every source is named. Every fact is verified. We do not hide behind phrases like "officials say," "sources claim," or "it is believed."

You will never read ambiguous, passive, or non-committal language on Arenar Intel. What you read is what happened according to who said it.

Short-form content is shaping the world. Arenar Intel leads that shift with precision, not noise.

What We Cover

Geopolitics. Conficts, diplomacy, shifting global alliances, and international relations.

Finance. Central bank decisions, market movements, trade policy, and economic developments.

Africa. Governance, security, confilicts, and development across the African continent.

BRICS. The evolving coalition of emerging economies reshaping global order.

Technology. Cybersecurity incidents, artificial intelligence policy, and major tech sector developments.

Our Standards

Sourced. Every piece of information is linked to an official or credible media source.

Verified. We do not report claims as facts. We wait for corroboration or confirmation before publishing.

Direct. We do not use ambiguous language, unnamed officials, or hedging. Every story names its origin. No hiding.

Sterile. No adjective. No alarmism. No narrative spin. Facts only.

Fast. Global events do not wait. Neither do we.
